How is the revenue share calculated for affiliates?

The revenue share is the percentage of the net gaming revenue. The gaming fee of the house win is of 20% and, as an affiliate, you're only required to contribute towards it, to the extent of your revenue share percentage. The calculation is made as follows: (Deposits – (payout pending + payout accepted) – 5% of Deposits in transaction costs)*Revenue Share % – (Revenue Share % x 20% gaming fee x Net Player Revenue)

Can you explain the "wysiwyg" model of commissions?

The "wysiwyg" model means that what you see is what you get. There are no hidden fees or deductions from your commissions. Also, the commissions are calculated separately for each brand. Even if your commission is negative with one brand, it won't affect the commissions from another brand.

How are payouts made?

Payouts are made when a minimum of €100 of commissions has been reached. Commissions are paid between the 25th and the last day of the next calendar month. Affiliate payments options include Bank Transfer, Neteller and Skrill. Please note that in order to verify your account and prevent fraudulent activities, the first payment of commissions is always processed by bank transfer. The following months, you will have the choice to receive your commissions payment via Neteller or Skrill.
